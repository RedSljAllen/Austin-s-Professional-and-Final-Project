When starting out in tennis, having the right equipment is crucial for both performance and enjoyment.

Here are some essential items you should consider:

  1. Tennis Racket
    • Weight Matters
    • Grip you feel comfortable with
    • Head size and shape has to help you hit the "sweet" spot
  2. Tennis Bag
    • Room to hold 2-3 rackets
    • Room for tennis balls
    • Room to carry water bottles
  3. Tennis Shoes
    • Good grip and traction
    • Comfortable fit
    • Durable and lasting material
  4. Tennis Balls
    • Regular duty for hard courts
    • Extra duty for clay or grass courts
    • Pressureless balls for practice
  5. Clothing
    • Moisture-wicking shirts and shorts
    • Comfortable fit for movement
    • Weather-appropriate gear (hats, jackets)
  6. Accessories
    • Wristbands to absorb sweat
    • Headbands for hair control
    • Sunglasses for outdoor play
